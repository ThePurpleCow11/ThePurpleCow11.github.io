Introduction

Thief of Moments is an epic fantasy story set in the world of Gaevara. Follow the journey of Leo and his allies as they grapple with powerful gods, dark secrets, and their own personal struggles.

Characters

Meet the characters:

  • Leo: A thoughtful, introspective individual with the ability to manipulate time. He struggles with his own identity and responsibilities.
  • Thea: A natural-born leader, fiercely protective of her allies, and armed with divine knowledge.
  • Caspian: The gentle water manipulator, driven by empathy and the desire to protect those around him.
